Cutting Boards

Various end-grain cutting boards made from premium domestic and exotic hardwood such as walnut, maple, purple heart, jatoba, zebra wood, paduk, etc. Each board highlights the unique grain and color of the wood. The end grain is not only visually appealing but also provides durability and a knife-friendly surface.

These functional yet artistic cutting boards are perfect for everyday use or as a stunning centerpiece for serving. Whether used in the kitchen or for presentation, they offer both practicality and timeless beauty.
