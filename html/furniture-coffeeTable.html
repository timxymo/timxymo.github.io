Entryway Table

Mid-century Japanese style entryway table inspired by Keith Johnson. The legs are connected by bridle joint, loose mortise and tennon, this is a pinnacle of hand craftsmanship. Made from premium Walnut, and two maple bowtie inlays add a hint of contrast to the top panel. This table adds an elegant touch to any home. Dimensions: 48"(L) x 12"(W) x 26"(H). \n

This piece features fine craftsmanship, sustainable materials, and a durable finish. Its simple yet elegant design makes it a centerpiece for any hallway.
