Jewelry Boxes

These mitered jewelry boxes are crafted from a variety of wood species. The handle, inspired by the design of a Japanese Torii Gate, adds a subtle touch of oriental elegance.

The box's body is carefully resawn to maintain a continuous grain pattern, often referred to as a 'waterfall' effect. The corners are reinforced with contrasting wood splines, enhancing both durability and visual appeal. This box serves as an excellent storage solution for jewelry and personal belongings
